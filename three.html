Gym Workout

Beginner | 3x a week(PPL/ Push,Pull,Legs)

Day 1: Push (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)

Push Exercises

Exercise 1: Barbell Bench Press

● 3 sets of 8-10 reps
● Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Exercise 2: Overhead Dumbbell Press

● 3 sets of 10-12 reps
● Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Exercise 3: Tricep

● 3 sets of 10-12 reps
● Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Exercise 4: Lateral Raises

● 3 sets of 12-15 reps
● Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Day 2: Pull (Back, Biceps)

Pull Exercises

Exercise 1: Barbell Rows or Lat Pulldowns

● 3 sets of 8-10 reps
● Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Exercise 2: Face Pulls

● 3 sets of 12-15 reps
● Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Exercise 3: Bicep Curls (Barbell or Dumbbell)

● 3 sets of 10-12 reps
● Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Exercise 4: Hammer Curls

● 3 sets of 10-12 reps
● Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Day 3: Legs (Quads, Hamstrings, Calves)

Leg Exercises

Exercise 1: Barbell Squats

● 3 sets of 8-10 reps
● Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Exercise 2: Deadlifts (Romanian or Conventional)

● 3 sets of 8-10 reps
● Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Exercise 3: Leg Press

● 3 sets of 12-15 reps
● Rest: 45 seconds between sets

Exercise 4: Calf Raises

● 3 sets of 15-20 reps
● Rest: 45 seconds between sets

Intermediate | 3x a week(PPL/ Push,Pull,Legs)

Day 1: Push (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)

Push Exercises

Exercise 1:Barbell Bench Press

● 4 sets of 8-10 reps
● Rest: 60-90 seconds between sets

Exercise 2:Overhead Press (Barbell or Dumbbell)

● 4 sets of 8-10 reps
● Rest: 60-90 seconds between sets

Exercise 3:Tricep Dips or Close-Grip Bench Press

● 3 sets of 10-12 reps
● Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Exercise 4:Lateral Raises

● 3 sets of 12-15 reps
● Rest: 45 seconds between sets

Exercise 5: Tricep Rope Pushdowns (Superset with Face Pulls)

● 3 sets of 12-15 reps
● Rest: 45 seconds between sets

Day 2: Pull (Back, Biceps)

Push Exercises

Exercise 1: Barbell Rows or T-Bar Rows

● 4 sets of 8-10 reps
● Rest: 60-90 seconds between sets

Exercise 2: Pull-Ups or Lat Pulldowns

● 4 sets of 8-10 reps
● Rest: 60-90 seconds between sets

Exercise 3: Bicep Curls (Barbell or Dumbbell)

● 3 sets of 10-12 reps
● Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Exercise 4: Hammer Curls

● 3 sets of 10-12 reps
● Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Exercise 5: Face Pulls (Superset with Reverse Flyes)

● 3 sets of 12-15 reps each
● Rest: 45 seconds between supersets

Day 3: Legs (Quads, Hamstrings, Calves)

Leg Exercises

Exercise 1: Back Squats

● 4 sets of 8-10 reps
● Rest: 60-90 seconds between sets

Exercise 2: Deadlifts (Conventional or Romanian)

● 4 sets of 8-10 reps
● Rest: 60-90 seconds between sets

Exercise 3: Leg Press

● 3 sets of 12-15 reps
● Rest: 45 seconds between sets

Exercise 4: Calf Raises

● 3 sets of 12-15 reps
● Rest: 45 seconds between sets

Additional Tips

● Adjust the weights to challenge yourself while maintaining good form.


● Track your progress and gradually increase the intensity over time.


● Include variations and advanced techniques for continued adaptation.


● Ensure proper nutrition and adequate sleep for recovery.


This intermediate PPL routine provides a higher training volume to promote muscle growth and strength development. As always, individual preferences and progress should guide adjustments to the program.


Expert | 3x a week(PPL/ Push,Pull,Legs)

Day 1: Push (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)

Push Exercises

Exercise 1:Barbell Bench Press

● 5 sets of 5 reps (heavy)
● Rest: 2 minutes between sets

Exercise 2: Overhead Press (Barbell or Dumbbell)

● 4 sets of 6-8 reps
● Rest: 90 seconds between sets

Exercise 3: Incline Dumbbell Press

● 4 sets of 8-10 reps
● Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Exercise 4:Tricep Dips (Weighted)

● 3 sets of 8-10 reps
● Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Exercise 5: Lateral Raises (Superset with Face Pulls)

● 3 sets of 12-15 reps each
● Rest: 45 seconds between sets

Day 2: Pull (Back, Biceps)

Pull Exercises

Exercise 1:Weighted Pull-Ups

● 5 sets of 5 reps
● Rest: 2 minutes between sets

Exercise 2: Barbell Rows or T-Bar Rows

● 4 sets of 8-10 reps
● Rest: 90 seconds between sets

Exercise 3: Hammer Strength Lat Pulldowns

● 4 sets of 10-12 reps
● Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Exercise 4: Barbell or Dumbbell Curl Variations

● 3 set
● Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Exercise 5: Face Pulls (Superset with Reverse Flyes)

● 3 sets of 15-20 reps each
● Rest: 45 seconds between supersets

Day 3: Legs (Quads, Hamstrings, Calves)

Leg Exercises

Exercise 1: front Squats

● 5 sets of 5 reps (heavy)
● Rest: 2 minutes between sets

Exercise 2: Sumo Deadlifts

● 4 sets of 6-8 reps
● Rest: 90 seconds between sets

Exercise 3: Bulgarian Split Squats (Weighted)

● 4 sets of 8-10 reps per leg
● Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Exercise 4: Seated or Standing Calf Raises (Weighted)

● 4 sets of 15-20 reps
● Rest: 45 seconds between sets

Additional Tips

● Prioritize progressive overload by gradually increasing weights or intensity.


● Consider incorporating advanced techniques such as drop sets, rest-pause sets, or cluster sets.


● Allow for sucient recovery time and listen to your body to avoid overtraining.


● Focus on proper nutrition, hydration, and sleep for optimal recovery.


This program is challenging and is intended for experienced individuals with a strong foundation in strength training. Modify as needed based on individual goals and response to training.
