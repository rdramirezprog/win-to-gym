Gym Workout

Beginner | 5x a week(Body Part Split)

Day 1: Chest

Exercise 1: Barbell Bench Press

● 3 sets of 10-12 reps
● Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Exercise 2: Dumbbell Flyes:

● 3 sets of 12-15 reps
● Rest: 45 seconds between sets

Exercise 3: Push-Ups

● 3 sets to failure (or modiﬁed push-ups if needed)
● Rest: 45 seconds between sets

Exercise 4: Incline Dumbbell Press

● 3 sets of 10-12 reps
● Rest: 60 seconds between sets


Day 2: Back

Exercise 1: Barbell Rows

● 3 sets of 10-12 reps
● Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Exercise 2: Lat Pulldowns

● 3 sets of 12-15 reps
● Rest: 45 seconds between sets

Exercise 3: Face Pulls

● 3 sets of 12-15 reps
● Rest: 45 seconds between sets

Exercise 4: Bodyweight Inverted Rows

● 3 sets of 10-12 reps
● Rest: 60 seconds between sets


Day 3: Rest


Day 4: Shoulders

Exercise 1: Overhead Dumbbell Press

● 3 sets of 10-12 reps
● Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Exercise 2: Lateral Raises

● 3 sets of 12-15 reps
● Rest: 45 seconds between sets

Exercise 3: Front Plate Raise

● 3 sets of 12-15 reps
● Rest: 45 seconds between sets

Exercise 4: Face Pulls

● 3 sets of 12-15 reps
● Rest: 45 seconds between sets


Day 5: Arms

Exercise 1: Barbell Bicep Curls

● 3 sets of 10-12 reps
● Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Exercise 2: Tricep Dips

● 3 sets of 10-12 reps
● Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Exercise 3: Hammer Curls

● 3 sets of 12-15 reps
● Rest: 45 seconds between sets

Exercise 4: Tricep Pushdowns

● 3 sets of 12-15 reps
● Rest: 45 seconds between sets


Day 6: Legs

Exercise 1: Barbell Squats

● 3 sets of 10-12 reps
● Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Exercise 2: Leg Press

● 3 sets of 12-15 reps
● Rest: 45 seconds between sets

Exercise 3: Lunges

● 3 sets of 10-12 reps
● Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Exercise 4: Calf Raises

● 3 sets of 15-20 reps
● Rest: 45 seconds between sets


Day 7: Rest

Remember to start with a weight that allows you to maintain good form and gradually progress. As you gain strength and conﬁdence, you can adjust the intensity and add more challenging exercises. Listen to your body and ensure proper nutrition and recovery for optimal progress.

Expert | 5x a week(Body Part Split)

Day 1: Chest

Exercise 1: Barbell Bench Press (Pyramid Sets)

● 4 sets of 12, 10, 8, 6 reps
● Rest: 120 seconds between sets

Exercise 2: Incline Dumbbell Press (Drop Sets)

● 3 sets of 10 reps (heavy), drop weight, 10 reps (moderate), drop weight, max reps (light)
● Rest: 90 seconds between sets

Exercise 3: Chest Flyes (Cable or Dumbbell)

● 3 sets of 15 reps (controlled tempo)
● Rest: 90 seconds between sets

Exercise 4: Push-Ups (Weighted)

● 3 sets to failure
● Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Day 2: Back

Exercise 1: Deadlifts (Conventional or Sumo)

● 4 sets of 6-8 reps
● Rest: 120 seconds between sets

Exercise 2: Weighted Pull-Ups (Cluster Sets)

● 4 sets of 5 reps, 15 seconds rest, repeat until failure
● Rest: 90 seconds between sets

Exercise 3: T-Bar Rows (Superset with Face Pulls)

● 3 sets of 10-12 reps, immediately followed by 15-20 Face Pulls
● Rest: 75 seconds between sets

Exercise 4: Single-Arm Dumbbell Rows (Drop Sets)

● 3 sets of 10 reps (heavy), drop weight, 10 reps (moderate), drop weight, max reps (light)
● Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Day 3: Rest

Day 4: Shoulder

Exercise 1: Seated Barbell Press (Rest-Pause Sets)

● 4 sets of 8-10 reps, 15 seconds rest, repeat until failure
● Rest: 120 seconds between sets

Exercise 2: Lateral Raises (Triple Drop Sets)

● 3 sets of 12 reps, drop weight, 12 reps, drop weight, max reps
● Rest: 90 seconds between sets

Exercise 3: Rear Delt Flyes (Mechanical Drop Sets)

● 3 sets: 12 reps (bent-over dumbbell), 12 reps (face pulls), 12 reps (reverse pec deck)
● Rest: 75 seconds between sets

Exercise 4: Shrugs (Giant Sets)

● 3 sets: 15 reps (barbell shrugs), 15 reps (dumbbell shrugs), 15 reps (upright rows)
● Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Day 5: Arms

Exercise 1: Barbell Bicep Curls (21s)

● 4 sets of 21 reps (7 bottom half, 7 top half, 7 full range)
● Rest: 120 seconds between sets

Exercise 2: Tricep Dips (Weighted)

● 4 sets of 10-12 reps
● Rest: 90 seconds between sets

Exercise 3: Hammer Curls (Drop Sets)

● 3 sets of 10 reps (heavy), drop weight, 10 reps (moderate), drop weight, max reps (light)
● Rest: 75 seconds between sets

Exercise 4: Overhead Tricep Extension (Superset with Barbell Curls)

● 3 sets of 12-15 reps each exercise
● Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Day 6: Legs

Exercise 1: Back Squats (Cluster Sets)

● 4 sets of 6 reps, 20 seconds rest, repeat until failure
● Rest: 120 seconds between sets

Exercise 2: Romanian Deadlifts (Rest-Pause Sets)

● 3 sets of 8-10 reps, 15 seconds rest, repeat until failure
● Rest: 90 seconds between sets

Exercise 3: Leg Press (Drop Sets)

● 3 sets of 12 reps (heavy), drop weight, 12 reps (moderate), drop weight, max reps (light)
● Rest: 75 seconds between sets

Exercise 4: Walking Lunges (Superset with Calf Raises)

● 3 sets of 12 reps per leg, immediately followed by 20 Calf Raises
● Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Day 7: Rest

This program is demanding and assumes a high level of strength and conditioning. Adjust weights as needed and prioritize recovery. Always listen to your body and consult with a ﬁtness professional if needed.
